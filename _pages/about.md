Welcome to my academic portfolio.
My name is Yildirim Gunes, and I am a researcher with an interdisciplinary background in Data Analytics, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Supply Chain Management, Social Media Analytics, Artificial Intelligence, and Land Use Planning (LUP).
Throughout my academic and professional career, I have been passionate about transforming complex data into meaningful knowledge and practical solutions. My research combines analytical thinking with real-world applications, particularly in Turkish Natural Language Processing, text mining, lexicon-based sentiment analysis, domain-specific text analysis, social media analytics, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making.
This website serves as a central platform for sharing my academic work and ongoing professional development. Here you will find my publications, conference presentations, research projects, and learning notes, particularly in Data Analytics, SQL, Power BI, Excel, Python, and related technologies. I also use this website to document my continuous learning journey in Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, Artificial Intelligence, and emerging technologies.
I believe that knowledge becomes more valuable when it is shared. Therefore, this website is not only an archive of my previous work but also a living platform where I will regularly publish learning notes, technical articles, project updates, and new research as my academic journey continues.
Thank you for visiting my website.
